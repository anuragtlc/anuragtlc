Vision

  • To be a Centre of Excellence to foster, to sustain and to enhance the quality of Teaching and Learning process.

Mission

  • To disseminate the best practices in teaching, learning and assessment among the faculty.

  • To develop an open and free-access educational and learning resources

  • To promote teaching and learning by engaging latest technological devices as aids to teaching-learning process.

  • To promote faculty development activities in Engineering and Technology, Sciences, and Humanities.

  • To promote engineering education research.
